What we have to offer!

in the Grains of Hope foundation, we offer a multitude of services

Providing clothes and supplies

We aim to provide clothes for those in need during season changes. We also provide home supplies and school supplies for people in order to highten their quality of life.

Mobile Health Clinics

We provide free moving health-care to people who cannot afford going or being inspected at hospitals. Our moving health-care clinics offer services such as surgeries, mild sickness treatments, free medicine, blood donations, and much more.

Meal prepping and providing

One of our main goals is to make sure whole families are being well-nutritioned.
